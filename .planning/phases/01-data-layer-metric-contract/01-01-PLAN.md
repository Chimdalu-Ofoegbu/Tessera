| T-01-03 | Denial of Service (self-inflicted, demo) | SPA routing on refresh | accept-with-control | `vercel.json` `/(.*)` -> `/index.html` rewrite so deep-links survive refresh; `/api/*` is auto-excluded by Vercel so the rewrite cannot shadow future functions.
